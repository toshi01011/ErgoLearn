Explore Ergonomics in Learning

Understand the key principles of human factors and ergonomics that improve learning environments, safety, and well-being.

🧩 Key Ergonomics Areas

1️⃣ Cognitive Ergonomics

Focuses on mental processes such as perception, memory, reasoning, and decision-making. Helps design learning systems that reduce cognitive overload and enhance understanding.

2️⃣ Physical Ergonomics

Concerned with human anatomical, anthropometric, and biomechanical characteristics. Involves designing furniture, equipment, and tools for comfort, posture, and injury prevention.

3️⃣ Environmental Factors

Examines how lighting, noise, temperature, and workspace layout affect learning performance. Proper environment boosts concentration, reduces fatigue, and improves overall comfort.

4️⃣ Human Error, Safety & Well-Being

Focuses on understanding human mistakes, designing systems to prevent errors, and ensuring safety. Promotes mental and physical well-being for optimal learning.
